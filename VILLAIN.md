## My Villain

He is me, but he has already hit $10k AUD/mo and is on his way to $15k. He doesn't need motivation. He needs less time in the day.

### Strength & Health

- Trains 5 days a week. 4 strength sessions and 1 pure cardio day. Never skips for "low energy." Treats fatigue as data, not an excuse.
- Body composition is visible proof of discipline. Below 12% body fat.
- Asleep by 10pm, up by 5:30am. Phone is not in the bedroom.
- Eats clean by default. Whole foods, protein-anchored. Doesn't negotiate with sugar.
- Annual bloodwork. Knows his testosterone, vitamin D, ApoB.

### Relationships & Network

- Talks weekly with 3-4 brothers operating at $30k+/mo. He is not the most successful in his circle. He is learning from people ahead.
- His partner knows exactly what he is building and why. She is a force multiplier, not a tax. They run the household like a team.
- Attends War Room IRLs. Known by name in his region.
- Polite to everyone. Close to almost no one. Does not waste energy on people who drain him.

### Wealth & Income

- $10k/mo is the floor. Already at $15k with two paying clients and a third closing this month.
- 8am-10am Monday to Friday is sacred. 30+ cold calls every day, no excuses. Sick, hungover, tired. Doesn't matter. Calls happen before client work, before meetings, before email. They are the first commercial act of the day. Monday's 10am and 12pm meetings do not move this block — the block moves them out of the way.
- 5+ active prospects in the pipeline at all times. Knows each one's stage and next move.
- EFA work generates $8k/mo and takes 20 hours a week, not 40, because he delegates content production, ad execution, and admin to Aussie freelancers at $30-50/hr.
- Sends 3+ proposals every week. Closes at least 1.
- Tracks MRR, pipeline value, and churn risk in a single dashboard. Looks at it daily.

### Mental Resilience

- Does not react. Pauses, then responds.
- Loses a deal, updates the pipeline, makes the next call. Doesn't spiral.
- Doesn't check social media before noon.
- Under pressure he gets quieter, not louder.
- Has had bad weeks. Hasn't had a bad month.

### Knowledge & Mastery

- Reads one real book a fortnight. Not skim. Real reading.
- Has documented frameworks for the work he does. He doesn't reinvent each client engagement.
- 30 minutes a day studying what's changing in his market.
- If a client asks a hard question, he answers it without hedging.

### Character & Heart

- Does what he says he will do. Every time. If he commits, it ships.
- Owns mistakes immediately. No deflection.
- Won't lie to a client for short-term comfort. Tells hard truths and keeps them as clients because of it, not in spite of it.
- Treats the barista, the prospect, and the brother with the same respect.

### Wisdom & Judgment

- Makes decisions fast on reversible things. Slow on irreversible things. Knows the difference.
- Reads people accurately. Doesn't project his own emotions onto clients.
- Knows the difference between urgent and important. Acts on important.

### Passion & Drive

- Doesn't wait for motivation. The work happens whether he feels like it or not.
- Has a clear 12-month picture: $20k/mo, two recurring clients off EFA, a second income stream.
- The work itself is the reward. Not chasing milestones to escape his life. Building because building is what he does.

### Struggle & Pain

- Trains hard enough that workouts hurt.
- Cold-called through enough rejection that it doesn't register anymore.
- When a client churns, it's data, not a wound.
- Will sit in a hard conversation rather than dodge it.

### Adventure & Freedom

- $20k+ in cash reserves. Makes decisions from strength, not fear.
- Could travel for a month if he wanted. The business doesn't need him in one location.
- Says no to bad-fit clients. Won't take work just for the money.
- Owns his time. Calendar is built around what he wants, not what others impose on him.
